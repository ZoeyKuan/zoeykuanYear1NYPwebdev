What are the types of readings provided?

GENERAL READING FOR THE WEEK

This reading gives you insights for the week ahead of you such as what to expect, what to avoid, what to look forward to, and insights for each day of that week. Other specifications like for insights for a week far in the future is allowed.

SPECALIZED READING FOR LOVE

You may choose your own spreads for a more specific question in mind regarding relationships. Available spreads for specific questions like:

  1. Future Partner / Soulmate reading
  2. Predicting when someone will fall in love with you
  3. Predicting your crush and how they feel about you

GENERAL READING FOR THE MONTH

Receive insights one month from now that consists of the common theme for that month, what may be causing blockages or challenges for that month, what are the potential opportunities for that month, and a insight of the 4 weeks represented by 4 cards.
Warning: Bigger overview, more general advice as such you may want to add on a special customized reading for more details.

SPECIALIZED READING WITH CUSTOMIZATION

As questions in this type of reading may not be listed, it will have a experimental type of approach and new spreads may be used. This type of reading should be the least accurate as I will have to learn new things for this reading which results in a longer waiting time and more irrelevancy.
